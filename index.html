About Us

Team Project Group 1
In the past decade there has been a significant increase in media sources as well as a growing interest in the political matters of the world. While it is important to be up-to date with todayâ€™s news, obtaining objective information from valid news sources can be time-consuming and often conflicting.

Say So Media offers a solution.

Our media portal allows you to search for a topic and generates a list of current articles and videos related to your topic. We provide a complete, comprehensive take on a singular issue by presenting information from different media sources known to promote both left-wing and right-wing politics.

We intend to highlight the complexity of political discourse by combining such opposing viewpoints. We ask that you please enter our site open to the possibility of evolving your views, and attempting to understand the political perspective of others. We encourage that you step out of your comfort zone and have a conversation.

Let us forge a more civil approach to discourse, one that honors both the inherent differences in our circumstances, backgrounds, and experiences, as well as the obligations and commitments we owe each other as members of one body.
